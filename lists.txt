% Mailing lists

[`pandoc-discuss`](http://groups.google.com/group/pandoc-discuss)
is for discussion of pandoc by both users and developers.

[`pandoc-announce`](http://groups.google.com/group/pandoc-announce)
is a low-volume list that is just for announcements of new releases.
